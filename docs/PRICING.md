# Evidentia — Pricing Model

## Value Metric

Per connected service per month. Each microservice or IT system connected to Evidentia generates continuous compliance evidence. The more systems connected, the broader the audit coverage.

## Tiers

| Tier             | Price                                               | Includes                                                                                       |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Starter**      | $750/service/month (min 3 services = $2,250/month)  | Evidence capture, audit trail, basic reporting, 3 users                                        |
| **Professional** | $600/service/month (min 10 services = $6,000/month) | Starter + incident tracking, M365/GitHub/Jira integrations, 15 users                           |
| **Enterprise**   | $5,000/month flat (unlimited services)              | Professional + Azure Entra SSO, custom evidence workflows, SLA, unlimited users, dedicated CSM |

## Pilot Offer

- 45-day pilot: 3 services connected at no cost
- Full evidence lifecycle (DRAFT → LOCKED) + audit export
- Weekly compliance posture report during pilot
- Conversion: auto-enroll in Starter at day 46 unless cancelled

## ROI Justification

- Failed SOC 2 audit remediation cost: $50K–$200K + 3–6 month delay
- Manual evidence collection for 10 services: 20–40 hours/audit cycle (quarterly)
- Evidentia Professional for 10 services: $72K/year
- Manual alternative: 1 FTE compliance analyst at $120K–$180K/year + audit failure risk
- Net savings: $48K–$108K/year + reduced audit failure probability
